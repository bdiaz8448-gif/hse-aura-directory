Terms and Conditions — HSE Aura Alerts

Last updated: June 2026

Service description

HSE Aura Alerts sends SMS text message notifications to designated safety leads and management contacts at the Fujifilm Aura Project construction site when an on-site emergency is reported, so building management can be notified quickly.

Eligibility

Recipients are added to this list directly by the site HSE Lead as part of their job role. This service is not available for public sign-up.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies and is event-driven (sent only when an emergency is reported).

Cost

Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-out

Reply STOP to any message to stop receiving alerts, or contact your site HSE Lead to be removed from the notify list. Reply HELP for support.

Changes

These terms may be updated as the program evolves. Continued participation after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
