Privacy Policy — HSE Aura Alerts

Last updated: June 2026

What this is

HSE Aura Alerts is an internal emergency notification tool used at the Fujifilm Aura Project construction site (Holly Springs, NC) to alert designated safety leads and management contacts when an on-site emergency is reported.

Who receives messages

Only individuals manually added to the notify list by the site HSE Lead, as part of their job responsibilities. This is not a public sign-up list, and phone numbers are not collected from the public.

Message frequency

Messages are sent only when an emergency is reported on site. Frequency is irregular and event-driven; there is no recurring or scheduled messaging.

Data sharing

Phone numbers and message content are not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Numbers are used solely to deliver emergency notifications related to this jobsite.

Message and data rates

Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-out

Recipients may reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages, or contact their site HSE Lead to be removed from the notify list.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the site HSE Lead, Baldemar Diaz.
